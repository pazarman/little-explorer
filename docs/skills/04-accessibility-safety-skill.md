# Skill 04: Accessibility and Safety

Use this to protect child usability and parent trust.

## Touch and Motor Accessibility

- Minimum effective touch target: 44px.
- Generous drag hit boxes and drop zones.
- Avoid precision requirements on small screens.

## Sensory Safety

- Offer reduced-motion behavior.
- Avoid stacking intense effects repeatedly.
- Keep error sounds gentle and brief.
- Support a calm mode profile.

## Parent Safety Controls

- Gate settings entry (long-press or parent check).
- Gate destructive actions (reset progress, clear data).
- Separate child-facing and parent-facing controls clearly.

## Accessibility QA

- Test on phone and tablet.
- Verify orientation resilience.
- Verify speech controls and fallback behavior.
- Verify contrast and readability for parent-facing UI.
