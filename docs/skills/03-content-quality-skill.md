# Skill 03: Content Quality

Use this for all in-game copy, voice lines, and prompts.

## Tone Rules

- Warm, calm, and encouraging.
- One idea per sentence.
- Prefer concrete verbs: tap, drag, match, count.
- Avoid negation-heavy language.

## Voice Script Constraints

- Instruction lines: 4-8 words preferred.
- Praise lines: short and varied.
- Corrections: specific and directional.
- Avoid sarcasm, pressure, or urgency language.

## Script QA Checklist

- Is the action clear from audio alone?
- Can a pre-reader understand the instruction?
- Is the correction actionable?
- Does repeated playback remain pleasant?

## Localization Readiness

- Phrase keys are stable and reusable.
- Dynamic tokens are explicit (e.g., child name).
- Avoid idioms that do not translate cleanly.
